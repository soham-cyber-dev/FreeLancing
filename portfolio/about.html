Tilak Ranjan Dhali

Tilak Ranjan Dhali is a professional photographer with over 8 years of experience in product and food photography across different genres. He is commissioned by various leading companies for his precise attention to every aspect of detail.

Tilak started his life as a mechanical engineer, but his love for his passion made him change his path towards photography. Having started his photography career in product and food photography, he is also comfortable and enjoys shooting across a range of other genres. He is always eager to create effective images in suitable artistic ways.

Based in Kolkata, Tilak shoots across India.
